    What is FinePortal ?


UiTM FinEPortal is a portal where it handles the financial information of students, staff and vendors at the campus. The users of the system are students, staff and vendors. Each of the users have different functions that they may use. The system provides many functionalities for students, staff and vendors. FinePortal and FinePay are examples of services offered that can be used by users. Other than that, UiTM FinEPortal also provides information through the system.
